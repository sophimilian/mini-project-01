## Brief description of the site
- Chic Parisien is a bridal and alterations website focused on helping brides and other clients request tailoring appointments.
- The site includes key service information, pricing basics, contact details, and an appointment request form specifically for alterations.


## Link to live site
- [https://sophimilian.github.io/mini-project-01/] 


## What I learned 
- I learned how to properly interview a client and ask the right questions to understand their vision, goals, target audience, and aesthetic preferences (which is harder than it sounds because people don't know exactly what they want until they see it).
- Building a website is difficult because client feedback is constant and requires many iterations; what AI generates for you at first needs repeated change to spacing, colors, typography, and layout to match the client's vision.
- AI is a helpful tool for generating initial code and suggestions, but it will never create exactly what you want—you have to be willing to customize, modify, and rebuild sections multiple times based on feedback to deliver a final product that actually meets the client's needs.
