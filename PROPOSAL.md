## My Project Proposal


**1. Client Brief:**
Client: 
- Catherine Fox Milian--owner of a bridal boutique, Chic Parisien, specializing in wedding dresses and professional alterations.
Purpose:
- The purpose of the website is to promote the boutique's bridal alterations services and allow customers (from in and out of the store) to request an alterations appointment online.
Audience:
- Primary audience is brides who already purchased a wedding dress and need professional alterations and anyone who needs any article of clothing altered. This includes brides who bought their dress from another store, destination brides traveling for weddings who may need last-minute tweeks, brides close to their wedding date who need tailoring, and any individual who may need simple clothing alterations.
Key Action:
- Believes that the most important action visitors should take is submitting an alterations appointment request. Customers can fill out a request form by describing what needs to be altered before their appointment is confirmed by the store. 

**2. Pages/Sections Needed:**
- *Home*: intro to the boutique and its alteration services
- *Bridal Alterations*: info about wedding dress alterations and what brides can expect when they walk in
- *General Alterations*: tailoring services available for non-bridal clothing
- *Pricing/Services*: bridal alterations prices ($1850 for wedding dresses) and note that other alteration prices vary depending on the article of clothing 
- *Appointment Request*: a form where customers can describe what they need altered before scheduling an appointment 
- *Contact*: phone number + email + any other ways to reach the boutique 
- *Store Hours + Location*: boutique hours and address 
- *FAQ*: answers to common questions about alterations, appointments, and timelines

**3. Content Status:**
- *Content we already have*:
    - bridal alterations pricing - $1850
    - store information including hours, location and contact details 
    - general description of the alterations services offered 
    - knowledge of the appointment process
    - ability to create FAQ based on the common customer inquiries 
- *Content we may still need*:
    - photos of brides + wedding dresses 
    - images of alterations work or maybe even inside of the store
    - exact questions to include in the appointment request form
    - logo and a finalized brand color references 

**4. Style Preferences:**
- *Vibe*:
    - Luxury, elegant, feminine, and modern minimalist
- *Color Direction*:
    - soft neutral bridal tones combined with black and white luxury elements. Subtle hints of baby pink will also be included to maintain a soft and feminine bridal feel...
- *Design Approach*:
    - The site should feel clean, polished, and easy to navigate with plenty of white space (very very user-friendly). The layout should feel sophisticated and not overwhelming, while still highlighting visuals and important information incredibly clearly.

**5. Inspiration Cites:**
- https://allurebridals.com/ 
    - this site has a clean and elegant aesthetic that fits the bridal industry well; the layout feels luxurious but still simple and easy to browse, which is the type of experience that she wants for her website and her users.
- https://www.pinterest.com/
    - it is visually focused and displays many many images while still feeling organized. I like how the site feels simple and not overwhelming to look at even withh a large amount of visual content on the screen all the time. 
- https://reesesbookclub.com/ 
    - this site is incredibly user-friendly and easy to navigate. The layout is simple, clean, and clear, which makes it easy for visitors to move around the website without feeling overwhelmed.
